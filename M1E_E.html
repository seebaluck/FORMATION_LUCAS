4) Mots essentiels

Today = aujourd’hui.

Tomorrow = demain.

Yesterday = hier.

5) Pièges

Les jours et les mois prennent une majuscule en anglais.

✅ Monday, January

❌ monday, january

Tuesday ≠ Thursday.

6) Exemples

Today is Monday.
Tomorrow is Tuesday.
Yesterday was Sunday.
My birthday is in July.
